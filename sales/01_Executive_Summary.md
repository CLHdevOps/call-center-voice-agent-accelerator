## The Solution: Grace AI Receptionist

**What It Does**:
Grace is an AI-powered voice receptionist that answers calls 24/7/365, handles initial intake conversations with callers seeking help with addiction recovery, and ensures every caller gets a professional, compassionate response.

**How It Works**:
1. **Caller dials**: Mercy House or Sacred Grove intake line
2. **Grace answers**: "Thank you for calling Mercy House. My name is Grace, how can I help you today?"
3. **Natural conversation**: Grace has a human-like conversation, gathering essential intake information
4. **Information collected**:
   - Full name
   - Phone number
   - City and state
   - Who they're calling about (self or loved one)
   - Reason for reaching out
5. **Seamless handoff**: Intake coordinator receives the information and calls back during business hours

**Key Features**:
- ✅ Sounds human (uses natural speech patterns, pauses, "um"s and "uh"s)
- ✅ Available 24/7/365 (never misses a call)
- ✅ Bilingual capable (English + improving Spanish)
- ✅ Handles multiple calls simultaneously
- ✅ Automatically logs every conversation with timestamps
- ✅ Consistent, compassionate responses every time
- ✅ Answers basic questions about programs, costs, and admission process
